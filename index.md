# Privacy Policy — LS Workout Tracker

Last updated: 19 May 2026.

LS Workout Tracker stores all of your data locally on your device. We do not
collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data with our servers or
any third party.

## What we store on your device

- Your workout programs (names, days, exercises).
- Your logged workouts (sets, reps, weights, timestamps).
- Your settings (units, accent color, theme).

This data lives inside the app's sandbox on your iPhone. Uninstalling the
app deletes it permanently. We have no copy.

## Analytics, ads, tracking

None. The app makes no network requests beyond what iOS itself does for
system services (e.g. App Store updates).

## Live Activities

When a workout is active, the app shows a Live Activity on your Lock Screen
and Dynamic Island. The data shown there comes from the same local storage
described above and is never transmitted off-device.
